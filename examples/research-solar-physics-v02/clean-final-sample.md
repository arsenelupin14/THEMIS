# Research Report: Limits of Machine Learning in Solar Physics

## Summary
This report analyzes the limitations of relying purely on data-driven machine learning models for forecasting long-horizon solar dynamo and convective processes.

## Research Question
To what extent can deep learning models capture long-horizon solar cycles without physical boundary constraints?

## Scope
Our review focuses on differential convective models and the propagation of error over solar cycles.

## Verified Findings
Data-driven models exhibit degraded performance beyond the training envelope due to non-stationary dynamo configurations.

## Recommended Next Steps
Incorporate physical regularization representing convective fluid dynamics boundaries.
